# Fossil Excavator

## About the Game

**Fossil Excavator** is a desktop idle/incremental game created for a 24-hour game jam.

You begin as a lone fossil hunter with nothing but a brush. Manually excavate fossils and sell them for money.

While not yet implemented, the goal is to grow your excavation site, discover increasingly valuable fossils, and build the ultimate fossil research camp.

---

## How to Play

- Click on the dig site to excavate fossils.
- Fossils are added to your inventory.
- Sell your inventory to earn money.
